Alexis Cordova's father, Michael Cordova is notable for willingly selling his child out to the Future Patriot Program. While some Americans came home from the Vietnam war believing that they should never have been there in the first place, Michael appears to have come home convinced that the USA had to do everything in its power to stop the Communist Threat.

Although it's unknown if he believed in domino theory before deployment, the question of whether his Cold War era beliefs were a result of his service are largely irrelevant. In the aftermath of his wife's death, Michael has ranged from a horrendously bad father, to a straight out attempted murderer.

There may be a timeline where Michael took care of his own issues, reformed his beliefs, and is no longer a ticking timebomb to those around him.

This is not that timeline.

Much like his extended family, Michael's two daughters do not talk to him.
